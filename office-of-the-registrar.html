Office of the Registrar

Overview

It is a central hub and vital arm of the institution serving clienteles such as students, parents/legal guardians, faculty, administration, and alumni of SPCF. As a repository of student records, the Office of the Registrar is both a source and resource of data that can be used for various researches, since the records are fully computerized and procedures are in order.


Vision

SPCF’s Registrar’s Office envisions itself to be a valued partner in the delivery of quality service to its stakeholders.


Mission

Provide quality service to clienteles with different backgrounds and perspectives in an environment where each is respected, valued, and welcomed.

Frequently Asked Questions

  2. Proceed to the specified offices stated in the clearance form for signature.
  3. ill-out the payment form and pay at the Accounting Office. Show your official receipt to the records evaluator who will give you the claim stub.
  1. The processing period for Transcript of Record and Form 137 is seven (7) working days upon request.
  2. The processing period for Diploma and Transfer Credential is one (1) month upon request.
  3. The processing period for Certification and GMC is three (3) working days upon request.

Present the photocopy of the document/s you are requesting for authentication to the Registrar’s Office. Fill-out the payment form and proceed to the Accounting Office for payment. Submit your documents along with the Official receipt to the Registrar’s Office.

The processing period will take three (3) working days upon request.

Present the original copy of the PSA Birth Certificate (for verification only) and Clear Photocopy (for submission). If the student has already graduated, he/she is required to submit an Affidavit of Change of Name and joint Affidavit of Two Disinterested Persons.
